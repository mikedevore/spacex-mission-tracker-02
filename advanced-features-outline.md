# Advanced Subscription Tier: SpaceX Mission Tracker Pro

## Overview
A premium subscription tier designed for hardcore space enthusiasts, aerospace students, and industry professionals. This tier unlocks deep technical data, real-time advanced telemetry, and personalized tracking features.

## 1. Advanced Live Telemetry & Mission Control Dashboard
- **Deep Telemetry Integration:** Raw data feeds during launch (velocity, altitude, stage separation events, engine status) where available.
- **Customizable Dashboard Layouts:** Draggable, resizable widgets allowing users to build their own personalized "Mission Control" view.
- **Multi-Mission Tracking:** Ability to track multiple simultaneous historical or upcoming missions side-by-side.

## 2. 3D Visualization & Tracking
- **Interactive 3D Orbital Map:** Real-time tracking of SpaceX assets (Starlink constellation, Dragon capsules, boosters) in low Earth orbit.
- **Launch Trajectory Modeling:** 3D flight path predictions and post-launch trajectory analysis.
- **Landing Zone/Droneship Tracker:** Live marine traffic data integration for autonomous spaceport drone ships (ASDS) and fairing recovery vessels.

## 3. Comprehensive Analytics & Historical Database
- **Advanced Rocket Metrics:** Deep dives into booster reusability history, turnaround times, and payload capacity utilization.
- **Launch Weather Forecasting:** Integration with rigorous meteorological APIs to provide launch pad weather conditions, wind shear graphs, and go/no-go probability.
- **Custom Data Export:** Ability to export raw launch data, telemetry logs, and historical statistics in CSV/JSON formats for personal analysis.

## 4. Proactive Alerts & Intelligence
- **Granular Push Notifications:** SMS and email alerts for very specific events (e.g., "T-10 minutes," "Propellant Load Underway," "Webcast Live").
- **TFR (Temporary Flight Restriction) Monitoring:** Automated alerts when FAA or maritime notices are posted regarding upcoming launch windows or booster splashdown zones.
- **Exclusive API Access:** A personal developer key for subscribers to build their own local scripts against our aggregated launch data stream.

## 5. Community & Archival Features
- **High-Res Media Archive:** Access to uncrunched, high-resolution mission photography and raw audio feeds.
- **Enthusiast Forums & Live Chat:** A gated community for subscribers to discuss technical details during live launches with other experts.
- **Curated Technical Reports:** Post-launch summaries focusing on the engineering and operational anomalies (if any).
